About Me

Hey! This is Paul from PS Hardwoods. I am 25 and just starting out trying to make a little business out of my hobby. I have been working with Paul Hopkins to learn how to make these things and now I feel confident in my own ability to make some quality products. I have made things from ornaments, coasters, and cutting boards. For sure check out my portfolio with the link at the top. You can check out some of Paul Hopkin's work too in the other portfolio tab. One thing that I consider our pieces unique for is our handles for our boards. I like to add a 45 degree angle handle that makes it really easy to grab a hold of the board. Look at my portfolio to see what that looks like. Though I highly recommend trying them out as I think they are the most comfortable handles that I have ever used on boards.

I also added my github account to the links page so that anyone who's interested can check out all of the websites that I've made since I've made a few other sites and not just this one. I have used the odin project to learn how to make them and would suggest checking them out.

Thanks so much for checking out my website! I hope you take a good look around and have a wonderful day!
