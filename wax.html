About Our Board Wax

Hey this is Paul from PS Hardwoods and ths is our homemade board wax. We make our board wax with all food safe materials so you'll feel safe with using it on anything you feel like you would want to use it on. Feel free to use our wax on any wooden product that you're using in contact with food. Like cuttingboards and things like wooden spoons or spatulas.

We make our wax out of mineral oil to bring out the grain of the boards or whatever you're choosing to use it on. We also use a mixture of waxes made up of beeswax and carnauba wax to lock in the mineral oil to make it so it lasts for many uses.

Using the wax is very simple. You start off rubbing some on with either a towel or your hands. Then you wait for at least 10-15 minutes but you can wait as long as an hour if you really want to have it be set in. Then wipe off the excess before use and then you're all set. Your curtting board should be all revitalized and ready fur use.
