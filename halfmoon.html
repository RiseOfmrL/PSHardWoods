This is a special board that I made for someone. It's completely unique and we had to use a special clamp to keep it together for gluing. It is made of purple heart, walnut, and maple woods.

The clamp that we used for this board is called a strap clamp. This is one of the first boards that I've ever made though luckily, I had some help from the other Paul featured on this website to make this work.

We have never made another board like this yet, but I'm sure that we'll try making another board like this again soon.
